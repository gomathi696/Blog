1. fxg7+

White plays gxg7+, capturing the pawn on g7. The move checks the black king and creates an immediate threat against the rook.


1. ...Kxg7.

Black responds by capturing the g7 pawn, the only legal move, since the g8 square is controlled by the knight.


1. h6+

White advances the h-pawn with h6+, delivering another check to the king.


1. ...Kh8.

The king steps back to g8, returning to its previous position.


1. Rxf8#

With Rxf8#, White delivers checkmate. The king has no escape squares: g8 is obstructed by the rook, while g7 is controlled by the h6-pawn.
